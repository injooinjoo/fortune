## App Review Notes (심사자 노트)

Dear App Review Team,

Thank you for reviewing our app. We would like to address the previous 4.3(b) rejection and highlight what makes our app unique:

**1. AI-Powered Facial Analysis Technology**
Unlike generic astrology apps, our app uses advanced computer vision and AI (Google Gemini 2.0 Flash) to analyze facial features. This is similar to how Co-Star uses NASA astronomical data for personalization.

**2. Real-Time Condition Tracking (NEW V2)**
Our app provides daily face condition analysis including skin tone, puffiness, and fatigue levels. Users can track their weekly patterns and discover personal trends – a unique wellness feature not found in similar apps.

**3. Emotion Recognition (NEW V2)**
AI-powered emotion analysis from facial expressions helps users understand their emotional state, with warm, encouraging feedback designed for emotional wellness rather than prediction.

**4. Social Sharing Features (NEW V2)**
Beautiful 1:1 share cards optimized for Instagram Stories. Users can share their "Today's Me" insights with friends – emphasizing community and self-expression rather than fortune-telling.

**5. Gamification Economy (Soul Token System)**
Users earn "souls" through daily engagement and spend them on deeper analyses. This creates a unique engagement loop not found in typical fortune-telling apps.

**6. Wellness Integration**
We've integrated meditation features, mood tracking, and gratitude journaling - positioning the app as a self-care tool rather than fortune-telling.

**7. Health App Integration**
Integration with Apple Health provides personalized insights based on actual health data.

**Key Differentiators from Existing Apps:**
- Real AI/ML facial analysis (not template-based readings)
- **Daily condition tracking with weekly trend graphs (NEW)**
- **Emotion recognition and wellness feedback (NEW)**
- **Instagram-optimized social sharing cards (NEW)**
- Token-based gamification economy
- Wellness features (meditation, mood tracking)
- Health data integration
- Warm, friendly communication style (like talking to a friend)
- Korean cultural heritage presented through a modern lens

**Target Audience:**
Our primary audience is women in their 20s-30s who are interested in self-discovery, emotional wellness, and social sharing – not fortune-telling.

We believe this app provides a unique, high-quality experience that differs significantly from existing apps in the category.
