===============================================
  HOW TO SIGN IN
===============================================

This app uses social login only (no email/password form).

Option A — Sign in with Apple (recommended):
  1. Launch the app.
  2. Tap "Apple로 계속하기" (Continue with Apple).
  3. Authenticate with your Apple ID.
  4. You will be redirected to the main chat screen.

Option B — Sign in with Google:
  1. Launch the app.
  2. Tap "Google로 계속하기" (Continue with Google).
  3. Authenticate with your Google account.
  4. You will be redirected to the main chat screen.

Option C — Browse as guest:
  1. Launch the app.
  2. Tap "둘러보기" (Browse) at the bottom.
  3. Note: In-App Purchases require sign-in via Profile tab.

===============================================
  HOW TO FIND IN-APP PURCHASES
===============================================

After signing in (Option A or B above):

1. Tap the PROFILE tab (bottom-right, person icon — the 5th tab).
2. You will see a menu list. Tap "구독 및 토큰".
   → Subtitle reads: "구독 플랜 · 토큰 충전 · 프리미엄 콘텐츠"
3. The purchase screen displays three sections:

   [Section 1] 구독 플랜 (Subscriptions):
     • "Pro 구독" — com.beyond.ondo.subscription.monthly (marked 추천)
     • "Max 구독" — com.beyond.ondo.subscription.max

   [Section 2] 토큰 충전 (Token Packages):
     • 10 Tokens — com.beyond.ondo.tokens10
     • 50 Tokens — com.beyond.ondo.tokens50
     • 100 Tokens — com.beyond.ondo.tokens100
     • 200 Tokens — com.beyond.ondo.tokens200

   [Section 3] 프리미엄 콘텐츠 (Non-Consumable):
     • 상세 사주명리서 — com.beyond.ondo.premium_saju_lifetime

4. Tap any "구독하기" or "구매하기" button to trigger the sandbox payment sheet.

5. To restore purchases: scroll to the bottom of the purchase screen
   and tap "이전 구매 복원" (Restore Purchases).

Step-by-step summary:
  App Launch → Sign in with Apple → Profile tab → 구독 및 토큰 → Purchase
