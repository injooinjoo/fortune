===============================================
  HOW TO SIGN IN
===============================================

This app uses social login only (no email/password form).

Option A — Sign in with Apple (recommended):
  1. Launch the app.
  2. Tap "Apple로 계속하기" (Continue with Apple).
  3. Authenticate with your Apple ID.
  4. You will be returned to the main messages screen.

Option B — Sign in with Google:
  1. Launch the app.
  2. Tap "Google로 계속하기" (Continue with Google).
  3. Authenticate with your Google account in the in-app authentication sheet.
  4. You will be returned to the main messages screen.

Option C — Browse as guest:
  1. Launch the app.
  2. Tap "둘러보기" (Browse) at the bottom.
  3. Note: In-App Purchases require sign-in.

  HOW TO FIND IN-APP PURCHASES
===============================================

After signing in (Option A or B above):

1. On the main messages screen, tap the PROFILE button

3. In the "구독 관리" section, tap "구독 및 토큰 구매".
4. The premium purchase screen displays three sections:

     • "맥스 구독" — com.beyond.fortune.subscription.max

     • "토큰 100개" — com.beyond.fortune.tokens100

     • "상세 사주명리서" — com.beyond.fortune.premium_saju_lifetime

5. Select any product card, then tap the primary purchase button at the bottom:
     • "구독 시작하기" for subscriptions
     • "토큰 충전하기" for token packs
     • "평생 소장 구매하기" for the non-consumable product

6. To restore purchases: tap "구매 복원" on the same premium screen.
